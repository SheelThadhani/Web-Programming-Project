Our Founder

"Why you pursue something is equally important as what you pursue. [...] Television news might just be something I love, as well as something I happen to be good at." - President and CEO Louis Bloom


Louis Bloom is the president of Video Production News, a news gathering service known for its hard-hitting investigative reporting and commitment to accuracy. Despite the cut-throat nature of the media industry, Louis was widely respected for his fair and ethical approach to journalism. Under Louis's leadership, Video Production News became a major player in the industry, breaking numerous high-profile stories. Louis's dedication to quality journalism was matched only by his commitment to his employees, whom he treated like family. His commitment to ethical journalism and his passion for making a difference in the world made him a beloved figure in the media industry and beyond.
